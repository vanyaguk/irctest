Our ways to serve one another

Sunday Services

Every Sunday we come together for a large gathering. The focus of our church gatherings revolves around the worship of God and the connections with one another

I-Teams

I-Teams are small groups of 10-15 people who meet up in person or online to chat, talk about the Sunday message, and pray together

Become a Volunteer

Start volunteering today and discover your purpose by making a positive impact while helping others. Regardless of your age or background, everyone can contribute to what God is accomplishing.

IRC Kids

IRC Kids represents the upcoming generation of leaders. Every Sunday childreno receive education about the love of God and the understanding of their identity. IRC Kids services take place concurrently with IRC Church services

IRC YTH

Young people from all our locations gather to receive inspiration, to strengthen their faith, and have an incredibly enjoyable time.

Church Pastor

EVGENY PERESVETOV

Evgeny and Olga Peresvetov are the pastors and founders of IRC churches in America and beyond it’s borders. They see the church as modern as possible and filled with bright colors of life. Their ministry is especially relevant in the family realm. For this, the «International Family Academy» was organized.

Also their passion is in restoring everything that is broken in the hearts and destinies of every person. In addition to serving the parishioners of the church, Evgeny is the founder of rehabilitation centers for people with any addictions. And Olga is the founder of the project for the protection of women and children suffering from domestic violence. To do this, Olga and her international team have established Safe Havens where any woman with her children can hide from abuse
